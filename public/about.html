  • What's with all of the video game references?

    Each of the games featured on this site have had some level of impact on me that directly affected my path in life.

  • Super Mario Bros.

    If my parents hadn't "lost" our old NES, I'd probably still be sitting on the living room floor playing this game to this very day.

    Super Mario Bros. was my first real trip into the world of video games (in combination with Duck Hunt, obviously).

    So, when it really comes down to it, my parents are responsible for getting me addicted to video games.

  • The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask

    I can say without question that this is one of my favorite games of all time.

    The eerie story. The creepy visuals. The INCREDIBLE soundtrack. It gave me chills in all the right places and I loved it.

    Sure, it's mostly the same assets as Ocarina of Time. But I didn't know that when I got it, and I didn't care when I found out.

  • Minecraft

    "Here's a bunch of cubes. Go build something. Or don't. Whatever."

    This was basically "LEGOs - The Video Game" before LEGO actually had a LEGO video game made. There was no story. There was no directive. You were - quite literally - dropped into a randomly generated world where you could either scavenge for supplies in survival mode, or go into creative and just build whatever you wanted.

    I built a 1:1 model of a NASA Orbiter. Because of course I did.
